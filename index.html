Our Story

Where Italian Passion Meets Czech Tradition

Every dish tells a story of passion, quality ingredients, and masterful preparation.

Located in the heart of Prague on Rytířská 22, The Theatre Restaurant brings together the finest of Italian culinary artistry and beloved Czech gastronomic traditions.

Locally Raised Meat with Care and Tradition — we believe that great food starts with great ingredients sourced with respect for nature and local heritage.

Our Source

Farm-Fresh Quality from Vysočina

In the heart of the Czech Republic, in the very center of Vysočina, you'll find a place that truly matters to us. Here, people, nature, and animals exist in harmony. We carefully respect the cycle of life and make full use of all available resources.

We work with respect and reverence for nature. Products under the Farmářská TEČKA brand meet the highest standards of both ingredients and craftsmanship. It's as if your grandmother made the pâté at home, your mother roasted the meat, and your uncle brought wine from the cellar.
